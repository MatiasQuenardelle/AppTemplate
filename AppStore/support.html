Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to Settings > Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to iPhone Settings > [your name] > Subscriptions > [App Name] > Cancel Subscription.

My data isn't syncing

Make sure you're connected to the internet. Try pulling down to refresh, or go to Settings and check the sync status. If the problem persists, try signing out and back in.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings > Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and all associated data. This action cannot be undone.

I forgot my password

On the sign-in screen, tap "Forgot your password?" and enter your email. You'll receive a link to reset your password.
